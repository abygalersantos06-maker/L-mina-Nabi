About Us

At Lúmina nabi, we believe every love story is a work of art, deserving to be told with beauty, light, and grace. Our name is born from two inspirations: lúmina, meaning radiance, and nabi, meaning butterfly—a symbol of transformation and new beginnings. Together, they embody the essence of what we create: luminous designs that celebrate life's most beautiful journey.

Each invitation and piece of stationery is thoughtfully crafted with timeless elegance, delicate detail, and the finest materials. Yet, we hold true to our promise that luxury should also be accessible—because love itself is priceless, and your wedding deserves nothing less than extraordinary.

From ready-made collections to fully bespoke creations, we weave your story into every design, turning fleeting moments into lasting keepsakes. With Lúmina nabi, you don't just send an invitation—you share a piece of your forever.

Vision

To redefine wedding stationery by blending luxury with sustainability, creating fine-art invitations — proof of craftsmanship and love for the environment. We envision Lumina nabi as the leading name in bespoke wedding stationery, inspiring couples to express their stories through artistry and purpose.

Mission

  • Art & Aesthetic – To deliver the finest balance between design, texture, and tone for that final snapshot of beauty.
  • Craft & Quality – Design that lasts; materials that feel luxurious to the touch.
  • Creative Integrity – Giving couples artistic freedom while offering cohesive luxury branding.
  • Sustainability – Embrace eco-conscious materials without compromising design or beauty.
  • Memory in Design – Create timeless visuals that feel uniquely personal to every client.
  • Service & Support – Provide an exceptional customer experience and help our clients feel seen, heard, and inspired.
